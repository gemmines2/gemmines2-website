Who We Are

Authentic Gems,
Honest Sellers

Welcome to Gemmines2, a premier destination for authentic, earth-mined gemstones. Based in Islamabad, Pakistan, with direct sourcing operations in Sri Lanka, we bridge the gap between the world's most famous mines and global collectors.

We were founded on one principle: absolute honesty. Every gemstone in our collection is meticulously inspected and described with complete, accurate information. We state clearly whether each stone is natural and unheated or has undergone traditional heat treatments — never vague, never misleading.

Beyond loose stones, we specialize in bespoke jewelry creation — handcrafted rings, bracelets, necklaces, and earrings set in 925 Sterling Silver, tailored to your specifications.
