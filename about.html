About Gemmines2

Gemmines2 is an independent gemstone business owned and operated by Yousef Zubair, based in Islamabad, Pakistan.

We specialize in natural gemstones sourced from Pakistan and international markets. Our collection includes garnet, sapphire, quartz, and other unique stones selected for their natural beauty and quality.

Each gemstone is carefully inspected and described to ensure transparency and accuracy. We focus on honest representation so customers can make informed decisions.

We provide worldwide shipping and welcome custom and wholesale inquiries.
