Who We Are

Small Business,
Big Integrity

Welcome to Gemmines2, an independent gemstone business based in Islamabad, Pakistan. We source natural gemstones directly from trusted miners in Pakistan and Sri Lanka and make them available to collectors and jewelry designers worldwide.

We started in 2025 with one goal: to offer genuine, honestly described natural gemstones at fair prices. Pakistan and Sri Lanka are among the world's most important gem-producing regions, yet many buyers struggle to find trustworthy sellers. We aim to change that.

Every stone we list includes complete treatment disclosure — we clearly state if a stone is unheated and untreated, or if it has undergone heat treatment. We also offer gemological certificates from reputable labs on request.

We also create bespoke jewelry — handcrafted pieces set in genuine 925 Sterling Silver, made to your specifications.

From the Founder

A Personal Message

I started Gemmines2 in 2025 because I saw a gap in the market — genuine natural gemstones from Pakistan and Sri Lanka, described honestly, at prices that reflect their true quality. Living in Islamabad gives me direct access to some of the world's most beautiful rough and cut gemstones.

I want to be completely transparent: Gemmines2 is a new business. I am building my reputation one customer at a time, one stone at a time. Every gemstone I sell is personally inspected and described with complete accuracy. I will never exaggerate a stone's quality or misrepresent its treatment status.

If you have any questions about any stone — its origin, treatment, quality, or certification — please contact me directly on WhatsApp. I respond personally to every message.

Thank you for trusting a small business. Your support means everything.
