Google Merchant Feed Generator

Gemmines2 — Generates feed.xml automatically from your products

⚠️ Important for Google Merchant: Never delete and re-add products. Always Edit existing products in the admin panel. Deleting resets approval history. This tool generates a proper XML feed that Google reads — your approved products keep their history.
Step 1 — Preview Your Feed
1
Click Generate Preview to see what your feed will look like. It reads directly from your live products-data.js.
ProductPriceStatusImage
Step 2 — Download feed.xml
2
Download the feed.xml file. Then upload it to your GitHub repo root folder.
Step 3 — Upload to GitHub & Add to Google Merchant
1
Go to your GitHub repo → click Add file → Upload files → upload feed.xml → Commit
2
Your feed URL will be:
https://gemmines2.github.io/gemmines2-website/feed.xml
3
Go to Google Merchant Center → Products → Feeds → click + → Select Scheduled fetch → paste the URL above → Save. Google will fetch it daily automatically.
4
Every time you add/edit products in admin panel → come back here → download new feed.xml → upload to GitHub. Google fetches it automatically daily.
