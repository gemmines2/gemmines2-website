About Gemmines2

Gemmines2, based in Pakistan, is your trusted source for authentic, unheated, natural gemstones — cut, polished, or river-tumbled. Our collection includes birthstones, rare gems, and minerals sourced from Pakistan and worldwide.

We specialize in custom-designed jewelry — elegant rings, bracelets, earrings, and necklaces crafted in silver, gold, or rhodium-plated finishes to reflect your unique taste.

Whether it's a wedding gift, a birthday keepsake, or a meaningful anniversary gift, Gemmines2 helps you give something truly precious. Worldwide shipping available.
