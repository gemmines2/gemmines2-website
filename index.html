About Gemmines2

Gemmines2, based in Pakistan, is your trusted source for authentic, unheated, and natural gemstones — carefully cut, polished, or in their raw and river-tumbled forms. Our collection includes birthstones, rare gems, and minerals sourced from Pakistan and around the world.

We also specialize in crafting custom-designed jewelry — from elegant rings and pendants to bracelets and earrings — in silver, gold, or rhodium-plated finishes, designed to reflect your unique taste.

Whether you're looking for a wedding gift, a meaningful anniversary keepsake, or a personal birthday gemstone, Gemmines2 helps you give something truly precious.
