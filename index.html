About Gemmines2

Gemmines2 is a Pakistan-based gemstone house dealing in authentic unheated natural gemstones — including cut and polished stones, rough crystals, river tumble stones, and personalized birthstones. Our collection is sourced both from Pakistan’s rich mineral reserves and from trusted suppliers worldwide.

We design customized rings, pendants, bracelets, and earrings in silver, gold, or rhodium-plated finishes — tailored to your personal style and preferences. Whether for a special occasion or everyday elegance, each piece reflects natural beauty and craftsmanship.
