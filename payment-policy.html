Payment Policy

Accepted methods, timing, security, and billing terms

Last updated: June 2026

1. Accepted Payment Methods

Gemmines2 currently accepts the following payment methods for all orders:

  • Payoneer — our recommended method for international customers
  • International Bank Transfer (SWIFT / IBAN) — for direct wire payments
  • JazzCash — for customers within Pakistan
  • EasyPaisa — for customers within Pakistan

All prices displayed on Gemmines2 are listed in US Dollars (USD). The price shown on the product page, in your cart, and at checkout reflects the final amount due — there are no hidden processing fees, surcharges, or additional charges added after checkout.

2. How Payment Works

Because Gemmines2 ships certified natural gemstones internationally and currently processes payments manually for added fraud protection on high-value items, our payment flow works as follows:

  • Step 1: Complete the checkout form with your contact, shipping, and payment method selection.
  • Step 2: Upon submitting your order, you will receive an Order ID and an order confirmation email/WhatsApp message containing full payment instructions for your selected method.
  • Step 3: Complete payment using the instructions provided (Payoneer transfer, bank wire, JazzCash, or EasyPaisa).
  • Step 4: Send confirmation of your payment (reference number or receipt) via WhatsApp or the confirmation form.
  • Step 5: We verify your payment, typically within a few hours (and always within 24 hours), and your order moves to packaging and dispatch.
We are actively working to introduce instant, automated card payment processing for an even smoother checkout experience. Until then, our manual verification process ensures every transaction is checked individually for security on both sides.

3. Order Confirmation

Every order receives a unique Order ID immediately upon submission, along with an on-screen "Order Confirmed" receipt and an emailed confirmation. This confirms that your order request has been received — final processing and dispatch occur once payment is verified as described above.

4. Security

This website uses a valid SSL certificate (HTTPS) to encrypt all data submitted through our checkout, contact, and payment confirmation forms. No payment card numbers are collected or stored directly on this website. Bank and Payoneer account details for receiving payment are displayed only on a dedicated, secure payment information page, accessible from the checkout flow.

5. Guest Checkout

No account or login is required to place an order. All customers can complete the entire checkout process as a guest.

6. Currency & Pricing Accuracy

All product prices are listed and charged in USD. The price shown on each product listing matches the price shown in your shopping cart and at final checkout. We do not alter pricing after an order has been placed.

7. Payment Issues or Questions

If you experience any issue completing payment, or have questions about an order, please contact us via WhatsApp at +92 336 214 9415 or through our Contact page.
