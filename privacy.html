Privacy Policy

At Gemmines2, accessible from Islamabad, Pakistan, your privacy is a top priority. This document outlines how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

To process your gemstone orders, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address. We do not collect this data unless you provide it voluntarily during the purchase process.

2. Payment Processing

Gemmines2 does not store your credit card or financial data. Payments are processed through secure, encrypted third-party gateways like Stripe. Your financial security is managed by world-class industry standards.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to fulfill your orders, provide tracking updates, and offer customer support. We may use your email to send a tracking number directly to you once your treasure is dispatched.

4. Data Protection

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with our logistics partners (DHL, FedEx, UPS) to ensure your parcel reaches you safely.
