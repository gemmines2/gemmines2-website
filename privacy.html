Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gemmines2 collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit our site or place an order, we may collect:

  • Name, billing address, shipping address, and phone number (including WhatsApp)
  • Email address
  • Payment information (processed securely through Payoneer or bank transfer — we do not store card details)
  • Order details and communication history
  • Website usage data via Google Analytics (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To arrange shipping and provide tracking details
  • To send order confirmations and updates via email and WhatsApp
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our website and services

3. Data Security

We take data protection seriously. Your personal information is stored securely and is never sold to third parties. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior. This data is anonymized and used only to improve our website. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Sharing of Information

We only share your information when strictly necessary to complete your order — for example, with shipping carriers (DHL, FedEx, EMS) or payment processors (Payoneer). We do not share or sell your data for any marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at yousefzubair03@gmail.com or via WhatsApp: +92 336 214 9415.

7. Third-Party Services

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
