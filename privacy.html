Privacy Policy

At Gemmines2, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our store or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

To fulfill your gemstone orders, we collect the following personal information during checkout:

  • Identity Data: Name and Surname.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, and shipping/billing addresses.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments to and from you and other details of products you have purchased from us.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly for business purposes, including:

  • To process, fulfill, and ship your gemstone orders safely.
  • To provide real-time tracking updates and customer support.
  • To comply with international legal, tax, and customs obligations.

3. Data Security & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share data with trusted partners necessary for our operations:

  • Delivery Partners: DHL, FedEx, UPS, and Leopard Courier to deliver your orders.
  • Payment Processors: We use secure, encrypted gateways (such as PayPal or Stripe). We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your cart items, and analyze site traffic to improve our services. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

Under global data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to access, edit, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at gemmines2@gmail.com.
