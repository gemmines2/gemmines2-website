Standard Return Policy

At Gemmines2, we specialize in high-value, 100% natural unheated gemstones. To ensure complete peace of mind, we offer a transparent return process structured directly around standard customer protections.

1. Return Window

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for any reason, including change of mind, completely matching our verified global merchant profiles.

2. Return Conditions

  • The gemstone must be returned in its original, unused, and unset condition with all original packaging, certificates, and documentation intact.
  • The item must pass our standard inspection. Any signs of wear, mounting, or alteration will void the return.
  • The customer is responsible for all return shipping costs, insurance fees, and associated import customs duties.

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for change-of-mind returns:

  • Custom-designed or made-to-order jewelry pieces.
  • Items that have already been set or mounted permanently into custom jewelry settings.
  • Bulk parcel orders or wholesale mixed lots.

4. Return Process

5. Refunds

After a successful, secure verification of the stone's original condition, refunds are processed securely to the customer within 10 business days.

6. Defective or Incorrect Items

If an item is damaged in transit or a distinct mistake occurred, we will cover the necessary return shipping charges and offer an immediate total refund or placement adjustment following verification.
