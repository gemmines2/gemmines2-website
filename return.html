Return & Refund Policy

14-DAY HASSLE-FREE RETURNS

At Gemmines2, your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not 100% satisfied with your gemstone purchase, we offer a transparent and professional return process.

RETURN ELIGIBILITY

SHIPPING COSTS

Please note that customers are responsible for paying the return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.

REFUND TIMELINE

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds are processed within 7 business days and will be applied to your original payment method (Stripe/Bank Transfer).
