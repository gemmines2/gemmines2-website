Return & Refund Policy

RARE & PRECIOUS TREASURES

At Gemmines2, we take pride in the authenticity and quality of our natural gemstones. To ensure a secure and transparent shopping experience for our global clientele, we adhere to the following strict return guidelines.

1. Return Window

  • Return requests must be initiated within 10 days of the confirmed delivery date.
  • Due to the high-value nature of our rare gemstones, requests made after this period will not be eligible for return or exchange.

2. Eligibility & Condition

  • Condition: Items must be returned in their original, unused condition, including all original packaging.
  • Documentation: All original laboratory certificates and grading reports must be returned. Returns missing original certifications cannot be processed.
  • Natural Variations: Natural inclusions, internal veining, and slight color variations are inherent characteristics of unheated stones and are not considered defects.
  • Security: Any gemstone that has been set into jewelry, recut, or altered in any way is ineligible for return.

3. Return Shipping & Fees

  • Shipping Costs: The customer is responsible for all return shipping charges and any applicable customs duties.
  • Insurance: For your protection, all returns must be shipped via a trackable service and insured for the full purchase value.
  • Restocking Fees: Gemmines2 does not charge a restocking fee for valid returns.

4. Refund Process

  • Once received, our gemologists will inspect the item to verify it matches our original high-resolution records and laboratory data.
  • Upon successful inspection, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

Thank you for choosing Gemmines2 — Your trusted partner in rare gemstone acquisition.
