Return & Refund Policy

At Gemmines2, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a transparent return process for our natural gemstones to ensure you are confident in your purchase.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Returns are accepted for damaged items or if the item significantly differs from the description.
  • Return requests must be made within 10 days of receiving your order.
  • Items must be returned in their original packaging, unused, and with all original gemstone certifications (if applicable).
  • Natural variations in color or minor internal inclusions are characteristic of natural stones and are not considered defects.

Return Shipping

  • The customer is responsible for return shipping costs and ensuring the item is insured during transit.
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable.

Refund Process

  • Once the returned item is received and inspected, a refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
