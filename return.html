Return & Refund Policy

At Gemmines2, we trade only in 100% natural, unheated gemstones. To ensure a professional trading environment, please review our policy below.

1. Return Window

We offer a 30-day inspection period. You have 30 days from the date of delivery to request a return if the item is defective or significantly different from what was ordered.

2. Gemstone Authenticity & Inclusions

By nature, natural gemstones may contain inclusions, feathers, or silk. These internal characteristics are proof of authenticity and are not considered "defects." We provide detailed descriptions and carats for every stone to ensure zero ambiguity.

3. Conditions for Return/Exchange

  • Defective or Damaged: If the stone is damaged during transit.
  • Incorrect Item: If you received a stone different from your order (e.g., wrong carat weight or species).
  • The item must be in its original, unset, and unworn condition with all original packaging.

4. Shipping Costs & Restocking Fees

Gemmines2 covers return shipping for defective or incorrect items. For customer remorse (change of mind), the customer is responsible for shipping costs. We charge No Restocking Fees.

5. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days back to your original payment method (Stripe, Bank Transfer, or International Card).
