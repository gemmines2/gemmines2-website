Customer Protection

Returns & Refund Policy

At Gemmines2, we specialize in high-value, 100% natural gemstones. We offer a transparent and fair return process structured around your complete peace of mind.

1. Return Window

We accept returns within 30 days of confirmed delivery for any reason, including change of mind.

2. Return Conditions

  • The gemstone must be returned in its original, unused, and unset condition with all original packaging, certificates, and documentation intact.
  • The item must pass our standard inspection upon receipt. Any signs of wear, mounting, or alteration will void the return.
  • The customer is responsible for all return shipping costs, insurance fees, and any associated import customs duties on the return shipment.

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following are not eligible for change-of-mind returns:

  • Custom-designed or made-to-order jewelry pieces.
  • Items that have been set or mounted into jewelry settings.
  • Bulk parcel orders or wholesale mixed lots.

4. How to Start a Return

Contact us before sending any item back. Email yousefzubair03@gmail.com or message via WhatsApp at +92 336 214 9415 with your Order ID and reason for return. We will provide return instructions.

5. Refund Processing

After successful verification of the returned stone's original condition, refunds are processed within 10 business days via the original payment method (Payoneer or bank transfer).

6. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If an item arrives damaged in transit or the wrong item was sent, we will cover all return shipping costs and issue a full refund or replacement at your preference, following verification.

7. Contact Us

For any return or refund enquiries: yousefzubair03@gmail.com · WhatsApp: +92 336 214 9415
