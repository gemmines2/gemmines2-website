Return & Refund Policy

At Gemmines2, customer satisfaction is our top priority. However, due to the nature of gemstones, we only accept returns for items that are defected or damaged upon arrival.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Only defected or damaged items are eligible for return.
  • Returns must be requested within 10 days of purchase.
  • Items must be returned in their original packaging and unused condition.
  • Natural variations in gemstone color, inclusions, or size are not considered defects.

Return Shipping

  • The customer is responsible for the return shipping cost.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Refund Process

  • Once the returned item is received and inspected, a refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
  • Please contact us before returning any item to confirm eligibility.

Contact Us

For return requests or questions, please email us at info@gemmines2.com.
