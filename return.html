Return & Refund Policy

At Gemmines2, we want you to be completely satisfied with your natural gemstone purchase. If for any reason you are not happy, we offer a straightforward return process.

1. 14-Day Return Window

You have 14 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return. To be eligible, the gemstone must be in its original condition, with all original certification and packaging included.

2. No Restocking Fees

We do not charge a restocking fee. You will receive a full refund of the product price, provided the item is returned in its original, unaltered state.

3. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping. We strongly recommend using a tracked and insured service (like DHL or FedEx), as Gemmines2 cannot be held responsible for items lost in transit during a return.

4. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the gemstone, we will notify you of the approval. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days back to your original payment method (Stripe, Bank Transfer, etc.).
