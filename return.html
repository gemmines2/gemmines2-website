Return & Refund Policy

PROFESSIONAL GEMSTONE RETURNS

At Gemmines2, we stand behind the authenticity of our stones. Please read our policy carefully regarding natural gemstones.

RETURN ELIGIBILITY & CONDITIONS

  • 14-Day Window: Returns must be requested within 14 days of delivery.
  • Original Condition: Stones must be returned unset, in their original packaging, and in the exact condition they were sent.
  • Natural Inclusions: As we deal in 100% natural, unheated gemstones, internal inclusions, veining, and natural growth patterns are characteristics of the stone and are not considered damage or defects.

REFUNDS & FEES

To cover administrative and inspection costs, all returns (except for items damaged in transit) are subject to a 10% restocking fee. Original shipping costs are non-refundable.

DAMAGED ITEMS

If a stone arrives physically chipped or broken due to shipping, please provide photo evidence within 24 hours of receipt for a full refund or exchange. External damage is the only condition for a 100% refund.

SHIPPING & PROCESS
