Return & Exchange Policy

At Gemmines, your satisfaction is our top priority. We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery, provided the gemstone or jewelry piece is unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase.

Custom or resized items cannot be returned unless faulty. For return requests, please contact us via WhatsApp or email before sending back any item.

Refunds are processed through the original payment method after inspection of the returned item.
