Return & Refund Policy

At Gemmines2, we trade in high-end, 100% natural gemstones. Due to the high value and logistical complexity of international gemstone exports, we maintain a strict return policy.

1. Return Eligibility

We offer a 30-day return window strictly for the following conditions:

Please note that natural gemstones often contain inclusions, feathers, or silk. These are natural identifiers and are not considered defects. We provide high-resolution descriptions to ensure full transparency before purchase.
