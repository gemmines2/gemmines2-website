Return & Refund Policy

At Gemmines2, we specialize in 100% natural, earth-mined gemstones. Please read our policy carefully regarding the unique nature of these items.

1. Natural Characteristics & Inclusions

All our stones are natural. By nature, gemstones contain inclusions. We categorize our stones as Loup Clean or Eye Clean based on these internal characteristics. Because these are natural formations, inclusions are not considered "defects" and do not qualify for a return based on "change of mind".

2. Eligibility for Returns

Returns are only accepted if the product arrives damaged during transit or if you receive the incorrect item. We do not offer refunds for customers who "do not like" the stone or have changed their mind, due to the high-security nature of gemstone logistics.

3. 14-Day Inspection Period

If your item is damaged or incorrect, you must contact us at yousefzubair03@gmail.com within 14 days of delivery. You must provide clear photos of the damage and the original certification documents.

4. Non-Refundable Items

Items that have been set into jewelry, resized, or altered in any way by a third-party jeweler are strictly non-refundable. All original certificates must be returned with the item to process a claim.

5. Refund Processing

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed and automatically applied to your original method of payment within **7-10 business days**.

6. Restocking Fees

Gemmines2 does not charge any restocking fees for approved returns of damaged or incorrect items.
