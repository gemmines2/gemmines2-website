Shipping Policy

SECURE GLOBAL LOGISTICS

At Gemmines2, we specialize in the safe delivery of high-value natural gemstones. Every shipment is handled with extreme care and is fully insured until it reaches your doorstep.

1. Order Processing (Handling Time)

  • All orders are processed within 1–3 business days.
  • During this time, we perform a final quality check and secure the gemstone in specialized protective packaging.
  • Orders are not shipped on weekends or public holidays.

2. Transit Time & Shipping Rates

  • Standard Insured Shipping: 7–14 business days.
  • Express Shipping (DHL/FedEx): 3–7 business days.
  • Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your destination and the value of the gemstone for insurance purposes.

3. Tracking & Insurance

  • Every order includes a Tracking Number, which will be emailed to you as soon as the package is dispatched.
  • All shipments are **fully insured**. In the rare event of loss or damage during transit, Gemmines2 handles the claim and ensures you receive a full refund or replacement.

4. Customs, Duties, & Taxes

  • Gemmines2 is not responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.).

Note: For returns, the policy window is 14 days from the date of delivery.
