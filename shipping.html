Shipping Policy

At Gemmines2, we take great care in delivering your gemstones safely and on time. We partner with trusted international and local couriers to ensure secure and timely delivery.

FREE WORLDWIDE SHIPPING

We are pleased to offer complimentary shipping on all gemstone orders. All shipping and handling costs are already included in the final price you see on the product page.

DELIVERY TIMELINES

  • Processing Time: All orders require 1–2 business days for quality inspection and processing before shipment.
  • Domestic (Pakistan): 2–5 business days via Leopard Courier.
  • International: 7–14 business days via UPS, FedEx, or DHL Express, depending on destination.

SHIPPING METHODS

We ship worldwide through reputable courier companies to ensure your precious cargo is insured and trackable:

  • UPS & FedEx International Priority
  • DHL Express
  • Leopard Courier (Domestic Pakistan)

ORDER TRACKING

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email containing your tracking number and a direct link to monitor your delivery status in real-time.

CUSTOMS, DUTIES & TAXES

For international shipments, customs duties or import taxes may apply according to your country’s regulations. Please note that these charges are the recipient's responsibility. We recommend checking with your local customs office for details.
