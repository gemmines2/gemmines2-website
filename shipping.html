Shipping Policy

FREE WORLDWIDE SHIPPING

At Gemmines2, we offer complimentary, fully insured shipping on all gemstone orders worldwide. We partner with the world's most reliable couriers to ensure your treasures arrive safely.

DELIVERY TIMELINES

  • Handling Time: 1–2 business days (Monday – Friday).
  • Transit Time: 7–14 business days (Monday – Saturday).
  • Total Delivery: Typically arrives within 8–16 days.

SHIPPING COURIERS

We use premium express services to guarantee security and speed:

  • DHL Express & FedEx: For most international destinations.
  • UPS International: For North America and Europe.
  • Leopard Courier: For domestic orders within Pakistan.

TRACKING & SECURITY

Every shipment is 100% insured until it is signed for. Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor your gemstone's journey in real-time.

CUSTOMS & DUTIES

Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties or import taxes set by your local government. These charges are the responsibility of the customer.
