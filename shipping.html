Shipping Policy

At Gemmines2, we take great care in delivering your gemstones safely and on time. We partner with trusted international and local couriers to ensure secure and timely delivery.

Shipping Options

  • Free Shipping: Available on selected orders and promotional offers. Delivery times may vary depending on the destination.
  • Standard Shipping: A reliable and affordable delivery method with tracking details provided upon dispatch.
  • Express Shipping: Fast-tracked delivery for urgent orders using premium courier services.

Shipping Methods

We ship worldwide through reputable courier companies, including:

  • UPS
  • FedEx
  • Leopard Courier (for domestic Pakistan orders)
  • DHL Express

Delivery Time

  • Domestic Orders: 2–5 business days.
  • International Orders: 7–14 business days, depending on destination and customs clearance.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with your tracking number and courier details to monitor your delivery status.

Customs and Duties

For international shipments, customs duties or import taxes may apply according to your country’s regulations. These charges are the customer’s responsibility.

Contact Us

For shipping inquiries or special delivery requests, please email us at info@gemmines2.com.
