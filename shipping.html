1. Processing Time

Orders are processed within 2–3 business days after payment is confirmed. You will receive a WhatsApp message and email with your tracking number once your order is dispatched.

2. Worldwide Delivery Time

We ship to all countries worldwide. Standard delivery takes 7–10 business days in transit after dispatch, depending on your location and local customs clearance. Total delivery from order placement is 9–13 business days.

📍 Dispatch Location: Islamabad, Pakistan  |  Business Days: Monday – Saturday  |  Cut-off Time: 2:00 PM Pakistan Standard Time (PKT, UTC+5)

4. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on your destination country, package weight, and selected courier. The exact shipping cost will be confirmed via WhatsApp after your order is placed. We aim to keep shipping costs as low as possible for our customers.

5. Full Insurance on Every Shipment

Every order is fully insured for the complete declared value of the gemstone. In the unlikely event of loss or damage during transit, we will file an insurance claim and arrange a full refund or replacement at your preference.

6. Customs, Duties & Import Taxes

International shipments may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, or handling fees upon arrival in your country. These charges are the sole responsibility of the buyer. We are unable to predict or control these fees — please check with your local customs authority before ordering if you are concerned about import charges.

7. Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, your tracking number is sent directly to your WhatsApp and email. You can track your shipment on our order tracking page or directly on the courier's website.

8. Delays

Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to customs clearance, public holidays, or local courier delays beyond our control. If your order has not arrived within the estimated timeframe, please contact us immediately and we will investigate with the courier.
