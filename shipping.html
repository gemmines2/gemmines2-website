Delivery Information

Shipping Policy

All orders are dispatched from Islamabad, Pakistan. We ship worldwide using insured, trackable courier services.

1. Processing Time

Orders are processed within 2–3 business days after payment is confirmed. You will receive a WhatsApp message and email with your tracking number once dispatched.

2. Shipping Carriers

We ship via DHL, FedEx, and EMS (Pakistan Post International), selected based on your destination and order value. All shipments include full insurance coverage.

4. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on destination, package weight, and selected courier. Exact shipping cost will be confirmed via WhatsApp after your order is placed. Orders over $500 qualify for free insured shipping.

5. Insurance

All shipments are fully insured for the declared value of the gemstone. In the unlikely event of loss or damage in transit, we will file an insurance claim and arrange a full replacement or refund.

6. Customs, Duties & Import Taxes

International shipments may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, or handling fees upon arrival in the destination country. These charges are the sole responsibility of the buyer. We are not able to predict or control these fees — please check with your local customs authority before ordering.

7. Tracking Your Order

Once dispatched, your tracking number will be sent via WhatsApp and email. You can also use our order tracking page. For any shipping questions: +92 336 214 9415
